
Chinese steamed buns.  Lots of options for shaping: Search youtube.  Also search for "ting mo"

Recipe via https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZmCXYjf0f6M .

100% AP (or pastry flour)
1.6% IDY
1.2% baking powder
6% sugar
54% water

Dough is very dry to work with.  Rest 15 minutes, then shape.  Portion 10 buns @
40g. Proof 1h. Steam 10 minutes.  The buns will want to stick to the steamer, so
they should be placed on tiny squares of parchment paper.
