

This is my emergency bun recipe when I need hamburger or hotdog buns for dinner
and I remember this at 3pm.

360g AP flour
220g water
7g salt
7g sugar
4g yeast

Mix and let proof for 45m in a small bowl in the microwave with a mug of hot
water. Shape into 6 x 90-100g buns and let proof 45m warm evironment. Bake 20
minutes at 425F.
