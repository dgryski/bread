
A tasty honey-oatmeal whole-wheat loaf.

75% AP
25% WW
16.5% rolled oats (soaked)
62.5% water
11% milk
7.5% honey
7.5% oil
2.2% salt
1.7% yeast

Mix as usual.  Bulk ferment 2h or overnight in the fridge, folding after the first hour.
After shaping, wet the top and sprinkle with oatmeal.  Bulk ferment for 1h-1.5h.  Bake
with steam for at 30-40m @ 450F.
